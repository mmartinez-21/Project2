Movies Page

Quick Info

If you've visited my page talking about my interests and hobbies, you'll know that I really like movies and shows. This page is just me reviewing a couple movies and shows that I thought are interesting, and are worth talking about.

Game of Thrones

I just recently watched the entire show over winter break, and I must say, it was awesome. I know that most people tend to focus on how bad season 8 was when thinking of the show, but it's also important to remember how good most of the other seasons were. When I started watching and enjoying the show, I was so glad each episode was really long. It was a great experience and although season 8 gave the show a trash ending, you can really appreciate the rest of it.

Signs

This is one of the first films that I have ever watched that truly made me feel so nervous and uneasy, but also invested into the story. I think this film is heavily underrated and knowing that many people prefer Shyamalan's other movies like The Sixth Sense makes me want to watch them as well. It's a really underrated movie and if anyone is into suspense movies, this is my go to recommendation.

Upgrade

This film really surprised me. I thought it was going to be a normal action movie, but throughout the movie, it felt like it was so much more, with the fantastic ending sealing the deal. I was really impressed. The best description I can give for this movie is that it's a better version of the movie Venom. The premise is pretty similar, and even the lead actors look the same.

The Lighthouse

This film was a spectacle, holy cow. Had some very awesome cinematography, very unique and gave off the creepy vibes that the movie was going for. Willem Dafoe was just awesome in this movie, his Neptune's curse monologue was so over the top and executed perfectly at the same time. If you're unsure about seeing this film, it's one of those films that's very open to interpretation, and definitely has a lot going on in between the lines.
